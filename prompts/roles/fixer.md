A rival reviewer already found issues in the implementation.
Now your job is to fix them completely without introducing regressions or papering over root causes.

Requirements:
1. Fix the stated issue, not just the symptom
2. Preserve existing valid behavior
3. Avoid introducing new edge-case failures
4. Add/update validation or tests where appropriate
5. Explain residual risk if anything cannot be fully fixed

Assume the next reviewer is hostile and will check whether your fixes are real.
